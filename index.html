Build Philosophy

Readable. Deterministic. Expandable.

I want player-facing systems, internal tools, and technical writing to stay understandable under pressure and strong enough to grow without collapsing into noise.

Core directives

  • Readable systems: the structure should explain its own intent.
  • Deterministic behavior: state and flow should be easy to reason about.
  • Expandable structure: growth should not destroy clarity.
  • Teaching through systems: writing and architecture discipline support the code, not distract from it.
